February was a breakthrough month. Instagram recorded 988,371 total views, with the February 19 brand ambassador introduction reel driving the majority of that reach at 913,965 views. YouTube generated 9,437 total views with 8 new subscribers gained organically. Facebook recorded 45,555 total views alongside 64 new followers, with a strong spike on February 19 as the viral content amplified across platforms simultaneously.
